================================================================================
                     FOOD DELIVERY APPLICATION - PROJECT SUMMARY
================================================================================

PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================
A full-stack React-based food delivery web application that allows users to:
- Browse and explore food menu items by categories
- Add/remove items to shopping cart
- View detailed cart with total amount calculation
- Place food orders with delivery details
- Login/authentication popup for user account management
- Responsive UI with modern design
